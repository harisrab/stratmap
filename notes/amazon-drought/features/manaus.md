# Manaus and Rio Negro gauge area

Manaus is both a major logistics hub and the place where river level measurements became shorthand for the drought's severity.

Manaus matters because it turns a sprawling basin story into an observable operational signal. When the gauge here falls to a record low, every downstream logistics assumption changes.
