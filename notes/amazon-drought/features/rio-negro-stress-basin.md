# Rio Negro low-water stress basin

Illustrative basin showing the wider part of the river system where low water translated into access stress for transport and communities.

The basin reminds the analyst not to collapse the crisis into one gauge reading. Low water affects a whole network, and different communities experience that stress differently across the basin.
