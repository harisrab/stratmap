# Western air corridors into Berlin

Simplified representation of the corridor structure that allowed continuous access from western Germany into Berlin.

Corridors look abstract until you consider how little slack they gave planners. Bad weather, aircraft spacing, and runway turnaround all had to be solved inside fixed traffic geometry.
