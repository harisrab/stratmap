# Tegel Airport

Tegel provided extra capacity and helped the airlift absorb the scale and rhythm demanded by the blockade.

Extra infrastructure changes a coercion contest. Tegel reduced vulnerability to congestion and showed that the Western sectors were adapting faster than the blockade could tighten.
