# Tempelhof Airport

Tempelhof became the most iconic visual symbol of the airlift and one of its most important receiving airfields.

Tempelhof matters because it turns political resolve into a throughput question. Every safe landing, unloading cycle, and departure was part of a repeated proof that the blockade could be outlasted.
