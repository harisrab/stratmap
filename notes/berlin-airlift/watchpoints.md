## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- Which infrastructure bottlenecks mattered most: air corridors, runways, unloading labor, or weather?
- How did the airlift turn symbolic political resistance into a measurable logistics output?
- Where can a map reveal the difference between strategic access and tactical throughput?
- How did the building of Tegel reduce pressure on the older airfields?
