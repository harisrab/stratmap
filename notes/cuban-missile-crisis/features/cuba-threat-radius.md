# Perceived strategic threat radius

Not a literal targeting diagram, but a visual reminder that the crisis was driven by the perceived compression of warning time from Cuban-based missiles.

Maps can help explain perception as well as hardware. The shock of the crisis lay in the belief that offensive systems in Cuba changed the strategic timeline in a dangerous way.
