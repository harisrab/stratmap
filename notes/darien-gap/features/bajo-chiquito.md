# Bajo Chiquito

One of the first villages reached in Panama after the crossing and a key point for immediate triage and aid.

Bajo Chiquito is where the route's physical toll becomes visible. People arrive dehydrated, injured, or separated from family members, and the narrative moves from transit to immediate survival.
