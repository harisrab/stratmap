# Darién jungle route

Simplified line of the jungle segment between the Colombian coastal departure area and the first Panamanian settlements.

The route line is where the notebook's human-risk story lives. Terrain, weather, criminal predation, and exhaustion all intensify once movement leaves the coast and enters the jungle.
