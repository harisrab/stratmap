## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- Where does the route shift when enforcement, weather, or violence disrupt one segment?
- How do reception sites convert a border-crossing story into a public health and child protection story?
- Which map features best explain why a record annual crossing count still depends on a few local bottlenecks?
- How should analysts separate the movement corridor itself from the organizations that profit from or respond to it?
