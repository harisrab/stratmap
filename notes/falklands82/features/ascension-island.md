# Ascension Island staging node

The island was the critical intermediate base that connected Britain to the South Atlantic theater.

If you remove Ascension from the picture, the rest of the campaign becomes much harder to imagine. It mattered because political resolve still needed an air bridge, refueling, maintenance, and a reliable place to reassemble combat power.
