# Port Stanley

Stanley was the administrative and symbolic objective whose fall would close the campaign.

The campaign only truly ends here. Once British forces approached Stanley from the west, the remaining Argentine defensive problem became one of exhaustion, isolation, and the inability to reset the fight elsewhere.
