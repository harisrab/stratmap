# Rio Grande strike base

Mainland launch point for Argentine air attacks that imposed continuing risk on British ships near the islands.

Rio Grande captures the distance problem in reverse. Argentine aircraft could reach the battlespace, but only with limited time and high pressure on navigation, refueling, and weapons delivery.
