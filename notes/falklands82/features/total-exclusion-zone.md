# Total Exclusion Zone

The 200-nautical-mile exclusion zone illustrated how a legal declaration could reshape operational behavior on the water.

This circle is as much about signaling as combat. It announced that Britain intended to turn the sea approaches into a managed battlespace and that entry by Argentine warships would carry direct military risk.
