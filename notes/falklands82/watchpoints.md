## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- How quickly can a distant task force convert sea control into a credible landing threat?
- Which points in the theater narrow the opponent's options to one or two predictable corridors?
- Where does weather or distance create a hard ceiling on sortie generation, medical evacuation, or resupply?
- Which political milestones mattered as much as battlefield actions: the exclusion zone, the sinking of Belgrano, or the surrender at Stanley?
