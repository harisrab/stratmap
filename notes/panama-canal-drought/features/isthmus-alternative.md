# Isthmus intermodal alternative

Simplified line representing Panama's short intermodal alternatives that gain relevance when canal slots become harder to secure.

Not every workaround is a full route diversion. Local intermodal alternatives become more strategically interesting once the main canal line is under sustained water stress.
