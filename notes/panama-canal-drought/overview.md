# Panama Canal Drought 2023-2024

![Drought conditions affecting the Panama Canal](/example-images/panama-canal-drought.jpg)
*Drought conditions affecting the Panama Canal's water system and ship traffic.*

> A climate and infrastructure notebook on low water in Gatun Lake, transit restrictions, queue formation, and how shippers adapted when the canal's freshwater system came under stress.

## Why this notebook works

This notebook is about the canal as a freshwater system, not only a transit lane. Drought changed draft limits, transit slots, and queue management because every lockage depended on water stored in lakes and reservoirs that were under stress.

Read the event sequence as a progression from hydrology to shipping operations. The map highlights Gatun Lake, the locks, the queue areas, and the intermodal alternatives that become more interesting when canal throughput falls. That lets the user move from a climate signal to a trade and scheduling story without leaving the notebook.
