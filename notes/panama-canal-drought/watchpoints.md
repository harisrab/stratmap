## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- Which indicators tell you the canal is facing a hydrology problem rather than a temporary traffic imbalance?
- At what point do draft restrictions and slot limits become a route-planning issue for global carriers?
- How do intermodal and regional alternatives gain relevance when the canal's main line is constrained?
- How should an analyst connect rainfall and reservoir levels to commercial decisions without skipping the operational middle layer?
