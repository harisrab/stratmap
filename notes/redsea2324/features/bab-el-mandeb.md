# Bab el-Mandeb Strait

The narrow southern gateway into the Red Sea and the natural reference point for almost every shipping security discussion in the theater.

This point condenses the logic of the whole notebook. Traffic, patrols, threat reporting, and rerouting all pivot around whether a ship can pass here with acceptable risk and predictable timing.
