# Cape of Good Hope diversion

Illustrative rerouting line around southern Africa used by operators choosing predictability over Red Sea transit.

This line is a commercial verdict. Once it appears regularly in planning, the market is saying that the chokepoint no longer offers a stable enough balance between time saved and risk assumed.
