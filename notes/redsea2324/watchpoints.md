## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- At what point does repeated harassment change the commercial baseline from acceptance of risk to sustained rerouting?
- How much of the response is naval interception and how much is operator self-protection through route redesign?
- Which ports and logistics nodes gain importance when Red Sea transit is degraded?
- How do separate missions such as U.S.-led escort operations and EU patrols divide labor or create overlap?
