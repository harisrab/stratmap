# Adré border crossing and reception area

Border town in Chad that became one of the main reception points for people fleeing Darfur.

Adré is where the humanitarian crisis becomes visible in concentrated form. It is close enough to absorb immediate flight, but too narrow to sustain large populations indefinitely.
