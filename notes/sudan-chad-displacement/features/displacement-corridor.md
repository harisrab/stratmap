# Darfur to Chad displacement corridor

Simplified movement line from violence-affected areas in Darfur to border reception and deeper camp systems inside Chad.

The corridor line makes clear that the border is not the end of the journey. It is only the first transition point in a longer humanitarian logistics chain.
