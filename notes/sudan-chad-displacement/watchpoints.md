## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- How quickly does renewed violence in Darfur translate into new surges at the same border points?
- Which sites are acting as transit points and which are hardening into semi-permanent camps?
- How does the rainy season alter road reliability, food delivery, and referral access for health services?
- What is the gap between the headline number of arrivals and the practical carrying capacity of eastern Chad's camp network?
