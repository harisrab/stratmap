# Suez Canal Blockage 2021

![Ever Given stuck in the Suez Canal](/example-images/suez-ever-given.png)
*The Ever Given stuck across the Suez Canal in March 2021.*

> A logistics and investigations notebook on the Ever Given grounding, queue formation, salvage effort, and the global trade ripple effects of a single chokepoint failure.

## Why this notebook works

This notebook shows how a single incident in a narrow engineered channel can cascade into a global trade story. The map is useful because it keeps the physical pinch point, the anchorages at either end, and the long Cape detour in the same field of view.

Open the event list to move from grounding to queue build-up, salvage, refloating, and convoy clearance. The actors are not military formations here; they are the ship itself, the canal authority, the salvage coalition, and the wider liner network that had to decide whether to wait or reroute.
