## Analytic watchpoints

Use these prompts to move from a static map to an investigative workflow.

- How quickly does a single physical blockage become a freight-rate and inventory story?
- Which parts of the map show the difference between a temporary queue and a structural route shift?
- How do salvage milestones translate into network-level decisions by carriers and cargo owners?
- What secondary questions emerge after reopening: compensation, legal liability, and canal resilience?
