# Bab al-Hawa crossing

The principal cross-border point for aid entering northwest Syria from Türkiye at the start of the emergency.

This crossing is the clearest example of how humanitarian geography can be political. A single crossing's status could shape the speed and scale of relief on the Syrian side.
