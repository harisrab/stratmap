# Gaziantep logistics hub

Major Turkish urban node and logistics center relevant for relief staging, warehousing, and onward movement toward the border and quake zone.

A disaster map is not only about destroyed places. It also needs the intact or partly functional nodes that can push aid forward, and Gaziantep became one of those anchors.
