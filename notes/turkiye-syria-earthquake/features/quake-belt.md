# Main quake impact belt

An illustrative impact belt covering the most heavily affected urban and peri-urban areas in southern Türkiye and adjacent Syria.

The belt matters because damage was not a single-city problem. Relief planning had to operate across multiple provinces and border-adjacent communities at once.
