# Türkiye to northwest Syria relief corridor

Simplified line showing the corridor from Turkish logistics hubs to the main cross-border entry and onward toward northwest Syrian communities.

The line emphasizes that relief is sequential. Stocks in Türkiye only matter if they can cross the border and then keep moving to communities whose roads, fuel access, and local responders are all under pressure.
